After Care Program

Most people think that after staying in a rehabilitation center for a certain period of time, treatment is over and the person is cured. This doesnâ€™t always prove to be true. The treatment of Drug Addiction or Mental Illness is a lifetime process where the client must follow certain rules & ethics and their family members have a very important role to play in this process. For that reason, we have developed a total guideline for a client and his/her family.

After-Care Services Include:

Regular Follow-Up

Regular follow up over the phone or in person to ensure continued recovery.

N.A. Meetings

Scheduled N.A. meetings to provide ongoing support and community.

Relapse Prevention

Planning and counseling to prevent relapse and maintain a drug-free life.

Recovery Literature

Providing books and resources to support a drug-free lifestyle.

Re-Union Arrangements

Organizing reunions for recoveries to share experiences and encourage each other.

Counseling Services

Offering personal and telephone counseling services to provide ongoing support.

Support for Relapse

Providing support and guidance in case of relapse to get back on the path to recovery.

After Care Program Details

Our after-care program provides comprehensive support to clients and their families to ensure long-term recovery. We offer regular follow-up sessions, N.A. meetings, relapse prevention planning, and more. Our dedicated team is here to assist you every step of the way in maintaining a drug-free lifestyle.
